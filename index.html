About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in pyhsical oceanography at Zhejiang University and work closely with faculties at the Second Insititute of Oceanography, SOA. I have a strong interest in exploring the effects of strong eddy mixing on the large scale circulation and coherent mesoscale eddy detection. I approach the problems by theory, big data outputs from numerical models, and observationsal data.
